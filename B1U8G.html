Reported Speech – Think Like a Reporter

Today’s Goal: Change speech and explain your thinking

35-minute lesson • Students discover the rule

Lesson Objectives

By the end of the lesson, you will be able to:

  • ✔ Change direct speech into reported speech
  • ✔ Identify tense, pronoun, and time changes
  • ✔ Explain WHY these changes happen
