Signs of Fake News
❔

No clear source

We do not know who wrote it or why.

🚀

Unrealistic claims

The promises sound too perfect or impossible.

🧾

No evidence

There are no facts, data, or expert proof.

📢

Too many ads

The page wants you to buy, not learn.
